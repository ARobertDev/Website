Who Is Elon Musk?

A Website by Andrew Martin & Luke Kwasniewski

Summary

Elon Musk is a wealthy entrepreneur, engineer, physicist, and father who founded SpaceX, Paypal, and The Boring Company as well as being the active CEO of Tesla Motors and Chairman of Solar City. At the age of 46, Elon Musk has a Net Worth of $20.8 billion. Elon Musk has 5 children, twin boys born in 2004, and triplet boys born in 2006. He shares custody over all 5 of his sons with his first wife, Justine Wilson.
